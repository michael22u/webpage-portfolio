Friendly Faces, 2016, 2021

Beyond Matter, 2015 - ongoing

It is an ongoing series of abstract watercolor paintings. Dissolving, changing and reshaping as a continuous process, as it happens in this world, is the topic of these pictures. The paintings are inspired by ancient Greek and Indian philosophical movements in which impermanence is seen as a basic characteristic of all existence. It is a world of energy that is in constant transition from one state of being to another.
