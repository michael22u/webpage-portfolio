Beyond Matter, 2015 - ongoing

It is an ongoing series of abstract watercolor paintings. Dissolving, changing and reshaping as a continuous process, as it happens in this world, is the topic of these pictures. The paintings are inspired by ancient greek and indian philosophic concepts in which impermanence is seen as a basic characteristic of all existence and beings. It is a world of energy that is in constant transition from one state of being to another.
