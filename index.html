Michael Ungerer

Visual Artist based in South Tyrol


The paintings, photographs and digital art of Michael Ungerer are of a subtle aesthetic that invites the viewer to look carefully at the picture. The spectrum of his artistic language ranges from abstract to realistic. Nature plays an important role in his works of art since he grew up in the countryside where he still lives today. The beautiful, the ugly and the mysterious about human beings may have their place in the pictures. Loosely spun narratives join them and allow for ambiguous interpretations. A thorough observation of the chosen image motifs precedes mostly his works of art. Not all of the pictures included here are accompanied by a descriptive or explanatory text. This is because in some pictures what is to be expressed is already fully expressed in the picture itself. Ungerer tries to create images that leave a lasting impression on the viewer and perhaps provoke a change of perspective on the subjects he depicts in his pictures. During his studies of contemporary art at the Free University of Bolzano, he participated in various projects that gave him an important insight into artistic practice.
