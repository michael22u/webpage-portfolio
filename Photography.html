Green Energy, 2021 - ongoing

A photo series (6 selected photos) that makes the wind visible in a tearing and pulling force. The images are charged with energy. The blurred areas in the photos have a resemblance to brush strokes, giving the images an irritating look, as if photography and painting have been fused into a kind of hybrid image.

Hall Of Thousand Pillars, 2020/2021

A series of abstract photographs that show dreamlike apparitions. Parallel worlds in which light, weightlessness and movement fill the space.

Having Hair, Premonition, 2018

Five people washing their hair at the sink or in the bathtub are the subject of this photography project. The people depicted appear slightly strange due to their posture, but at the same time they establish a subtle intimacy with the viewer. Tension and relaxation merge into one another.
